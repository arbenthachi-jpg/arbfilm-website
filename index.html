ABOUT ARBFILM

Independent Cinema With A Strong Visual Identity

ARBFILM is an independent film production company based in Tetovo, North Macedonia, founded by filmmaker Arben Thaçi. The company develops and produces feature films, documentaries, commercial productions and cinematic visual stories.

OUR APPROACH

Cinema Rooted in Memory, Landscape and Human Conflict

ARBFILM develops stories that explore identity, history, morality and the invisible forces that shape human life. Our work combines intimate character drama with strong visual atmosphere, using the landscapes of the Balkans as emotional and cinematic spaces.

WHAT WE DO

From Development to Production

We work across feature films, documentaries, commercials and cultural projects, offering creative development, production coordination, cinematography support and professional cinema equipment rental for local and international productions.
